What is Idrive 360?

Idrive 360 is designed for enterprise users to back up data from employee devices (endpoints), manage users, assign policies, and monitor backup status—all from one central dashboard. It is especially useful for companies with remote teams or multiple office locations.

How Do I Login to Idrive 360?

  1. Go to the login page: idrive 360 login
  2. Enter your admin email address and password.
  3. Click the “Login” button to access the 360 Admin Console.
  4. Navigate the dashboard to manage users, endpoints, and backup plans.

Key Features After Login

  • Dashboard Overview: Real-time status of all connected devices and users.
  • Group Management: Assign users to groups and set policies easily.
  • Activity Logs: Monitor backups, failures, and changes.
  • Remote Deployment: Install backup clients across multiple devices remotely.

How to Set Up Users in Idrive 360

  1. Log in to the admin dashboard.
  2. Go to the “Users” tab and click on “Add User”.
  3. Fill in their email and assign them to a group or plan.
  4. Users will receive an email with setup instructions.

Troubleshooting Login Issues

  • Invalid Credentials: Double-check your email and password. Use "Forgot Password" if needed.
  • Server Timeout: Try a different browser or incognito mode to bypass cache problems.
  • Blocked Login: Check firewall or network restrictions on your company device.
  • Account Not Found: Ensure you are using the correct Idrive 360 admin login, not personal Idrive login.

Benefits of Using Idrive 360

  • Centralized control of data backup for all employee devices
  • Simple user onboarding and policy management
  • Detailed audit trails and compliance reports
  • Affordable, scalable plans for growing teams
